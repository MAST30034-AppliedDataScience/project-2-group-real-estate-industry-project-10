# Datasets
Please provide a high level `README.md` for your chosen datasets
In general, our datasets are divided into three parts:
(1):Domain data: This is the main rent data used to train our ARIMA model and random forest model.
(2):Historical data: This is used as the training set to predict in our ARIMA model.
(3):External data: External data contains the geospatial data and rent price data collected from ABS website and google map: Park, CBD_distance, hospital and schools, station and malls, income and crime_rate, population.
